Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 30, 2026

La Boîte à Chaussures ("Pictures Box") is a photo viewer application for Windows 10/11. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Overview

Your privacy is important to us. This app is designed to work entirely on your device without collecting or transmitting your personal information.

Information We Access

📸 Photos Access

  • The app accesses your Pictures Library to display your photos
  • All photos remain on your device - nothing is uploaded or transmitted
  • The app only reads photo files; it does not modify or delete your photos
  • Photo access requires your explicit permission via Windows permissions

💾 Local Data Storage

  • The app stores a local index of your photos to improve performance
  • This data is stored only on your device in the app's local storage folder
  • No personal data is sent to external servers

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ We do not collect personal information
  • ❌ We do not upload your photos anywhere
  • ❌ We do not track your usage
  • ❌ We do not share data with third parties
  • ❌ We do not use analytics or advertising services
  • ❌ No internet connection is required

🔒 Data Security

  • All data remains local on your Windows device
  • The app uses Windows standard security and permissions
  • No internet connection is required for the app to function
  • Your photos never leave your device

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Revoke photo library access at any time in Windows Settings → Privacy → Pictures
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Request information about your data by contacting us

👶 Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
